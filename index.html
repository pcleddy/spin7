How to play: On your turn, 8 unique playable letters appear. Type any valid English word using those letters — you can reuse the same letter as many times as you like. A ★ joker is worth 0 points, can stand in for one missing letter family, and that letter only scores once. Score = sum of letter values, plus 2 bonus points for each letter beyond 7. First to the target wins that meta-round; most meta-round wins takes the match.
